A talk given by Son Hoang about the Event-B platform.

# Abstract
Event-B is a formal modelling method supporting correct-by-construction development via the use of refinement to introduce details into the models gradually. It is an evolution from the (classical) B Method underpinning industrial work such as the Paris Metro Line 14. In this talk, I will provide a brief introduction to Event-B and its supporting Rodin Platform, illustrated with examples from system modelling. (This will be a slide-free talk with some tool demos.)
